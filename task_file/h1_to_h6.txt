Task 1: Personal Profile Page
Goal: Create headings for your personal profile.

Requirements:

<h1>: Your Name
<h2>: About Me
<h3>: Education
<h3>: Skills
<h2>: Contact

Example (replace with your info!):

<h1>Kenil Patel</h1>
<h2>About Me</h2>
<h3>Education</h3>
<h3>Skills</h3>
<h2>Contact</h2>

Task 2: Blog Post Outline
Goal: Structure a blog post with headings.

Requirements:

<h1>: Blog Title (e.g., “My Travel Blog”)
<h2>: Introduction
<h2>: Destinations
<h3>: India
<h3>: Japan
<h2>: Conclusion

Example:

<h1>My Travel Blog</h1>
<h2>Introduction</h2>
<h2>Destinations</h2>
<h3>India</h3>
<h3>Japan</h3>
<h2>Conclusion</h2>
Task 3: School Project Page
Goal: Design headings for a school project webpage.

Requirements:

<h1>: Project Title
<h2>: Problem Statement
<h2>: Methodology
<h2>: Results
<h2>: Conclusion
<h3>: Future Work (inside Conclusion)

Example:

<h1>Water Conservation Project</h1>
<h2>Problem Statement</h2>
<h2>Methodology</h2>
<h2>Results</h2>
<h2>Conclusion</h2>
<h3>Future Work</h3>
